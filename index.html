About me

I’m a Computer Engineering student at Texas A&M. I like building systems that are easy to use and reliable. This semester I want to focus on how accessibility fits into the design process early—before it becomes a last-minute fix.

I’m especially interested in accessibility for people who use assistive technology, and in designing interfaces that work well with keyboards, screen readers, and different visual needs.

What I’m hoping to learn

  • How to design for screen reader + keyboard users from the start
  • How to evaluate accessibility and communicate issues clearly
