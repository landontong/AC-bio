About me

I’m a Computer Engineering student at Texas A&M. I like working on projects that mix hardware and software, but I’ve been trying to pay more attention to accessibility instead of treating it like an “extra.”

For accessibility, I’m mostly interested in stuff that makes websites/apps actually work for keyboard-only users and screen readers, and also small design choices that help people with low vision.

What I’m trying to get better at

  • Designing pages that make sense with a screen reader
  • Making forms and navigation usable without a mouse

I’d be excited to work on something like an accessibility checklist tool, a small web app that’s designed accessibility-first, or anything that improves a common student workflow (registration, planning, studying) in a more inclusive way.

If you’re into similar things, let’s team up. I’m especially interested in projects that are simple but done “the right way.”
